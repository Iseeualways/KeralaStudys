About Kerala Study Pvt. Ltd.ur

Kerala Study is an educational services provider that has been active in the education sector since 1994. Our headquarters is optimally located in Bangalore, with a strong network of branches across India, including Kerala and Haryana, as well as international presence in the UAE, Bahrain, and Qatar.

Our primary focus is on delivering accurate academic guidance through seasoned counsellors who possess in-depth knowledge of the education industry. We predominantly support students in fields such as Medicine, Dentistry, Engineering, Computer Science, Nursing, Pharmacy, Law, Management, and more.

Our services are not limited by nationality. We cater to Indian nationals, NRIs, and international students, with a commitment to making quality academic assistance accessible to all. In addition to providing admission guidance for regional institutions, we also help students pursue higher education opportunities at some of the world’s top universities and colleges.
